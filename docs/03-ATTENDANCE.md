# Attendance System

## Overview

The Attendance System tracks employee working hours, presence, and absences. It provides both employees and administrators with tools to record and monitor attendance accurately.

## Purpose

The Attendance System is designed to:
- Record daily employee check-in and check-out times
- Monitor employee presence and punctuality
- Generate attendance reports and statistics
- Identify attendance patterns and trends
- Support payroll and performance management

## What Users Can See

### For Employees

#### Employee Attendance View
- **Today's Status**: Current check-in/check-out status
- **Attendance Button**: Quick check-in/check-out functionality
- **Personal Attendance History**: View your own attendance records
- **Monthly Summary**: Overview of your attendance for the month
- **Attendance Statistics**: Your punctuality and presence metrics

### For Administrators

#### Admin Attendance Dashboard
- **All Employee Attendance**: Complete attendance records for all staff
- **Today's Attendance**: Real-time view of who is present
- **Attendance Summary Cards**: 
  - Total employees present today
  - Total employees absent today
  - Late arrivals
  - Early departures
- **Attendance Calendar**: Visual attendance grid
- **Search and Filter Options**: Find specific records
- **Export Functions**: Download attendance reports

## How to Use the Attendance System

### For Employees: Recording Attendance

#### Daily Check-In

1. **Login** to the HR Portal
2. Navigate to **"Attendance"** from your dashboard
3. Click the **"Check In"** button
4. System automatically records:
   - Date
   - Check-in time
   - Your location (if enabled)
5. Confirmation message appears
6. Status updates to "Checked In"

#### Daily Check-Out

1. At the end of your workday, navigate to **"Attendance"**
2. Click the **"Check Out"** button
3. System records:
   - Check-out time
   - Total hours worked
   - Location (if enabled)
4. Confirmation message displayed
5. Daily attendance record is complete

#### Viewing Your Attendance

1. Go to **"Attendance"** section
2. View your attendance records in a table format:
   - Date
   - Check-in time
   - Check-out time
   - Total hours
   - Status (Present/Absent/Late/Half-day)
3. Use **date filters** to view specific periods
4. Click **"Export"** to download your records

## Attendance Features

### Automatic Time Recording
- System timestamps check-in/check-out automatically
- No manual time entry required
- Prevents time manipulation

### Geo-location (Optional)
- Records employee location during check-in/check-out
- Ensures on-site presence verification
- Can be enabled/disabled by admin

### Status Categories

The system categorizes attendance as:
- **Present**: Normal check-in and check-out
- **Absent**: No check-in recorded
- **Late**: Check-in after scheduled start time
- **Half-day**: Partial day attendance
- **On Leave**: Approved leave for the day
- **Holiday**: Public/company holiday

### Notifications

Automated notifications for:
- **Employees**: 
  - Reminder to check in (if late)
  - Reminder to check out (end of day)
  - Attendance corrections
- **Administrators**:
  - Daily attendance summary
  - Unusual patterns detected
  - Multiple absences alert

### Integration with Leave System

- Approved leaves automatically mark attendance
- No check-in required on leave days
- Leave days don't count as absent
- Seamless data synchronization

## Attendance Policies

### Working Hours
- Standard working hours configured in system
- Flexible timing options (if applicable)
- Grace period for late check-in
- Overtime tracking

### Late Arrivals
- Defined grace period (e.g., 15 minutes)
- Marked as late after grace period
- Multiple late arrivals may trigger alerts

### Absence Tracking
- Consecutive absences flagged
- Unplanned absences require explanation
- Patterns reported to supervisors

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"Unable to Check In" Error**
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you're on the correct network (if restricted)
- Clear browser cache and retry
- Contact IT support if persists

**Missing Attendance Record**
- Verify you clicked "Check In" button
- Check if system was down during that time
- Contact admin for manual entry

**Incorrect Time Recorded**
- Note the discrepancy immediately
- Contact your supervisor or HR
- Request attendance correction
- Admin can manually adjust

**Location Not Detected**
- Enable location services in browser
- Allow location access for the portal
- Check device GPS settings
- Contact IT if problem continues
